Raj & Manjit — sisters & owners

Our Story

Two Sisters,
One Kitchen Table Dream

Manjit and Raj have called Central Texas home for decades. They know what it's like to drive hours for the right spice, or wait for family to bring things back in their suitcases. So in 2015, they opened the doors to Om Spice & Sweets.

It's still a small store — just two sisters, side by side. No big chain, no corporate backing. Just family, taking care of family. Step inside and you'll find one of them at the counter, ready to ask how your family's doing and which chili powder you actually need.
